## Ingredients

- 1 tsp _sugar_
- 1 tbsp _hot water_
- 2 tbsp _soy sauce_
- 1 tsp _rice vinegar_
- 1 tsp _chili oil_
- 1 tsp _garlic_, minced
- 1 tsp toasted _sesame seeds_
- 1/2 tsp _sesame oil_

## Directions

1. Dissolve sugar in hot water.
2. Stir in soy sauce, rice vinegar, chili oil, garlic, sesame seeds, and sesame oil.
