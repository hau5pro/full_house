## Ingredients

- 3 _garlic_ cloves
- 3/4 cup _mayonnaise_ (full-fat)
- 2 tbsp plain _greek yogurt_
- 2 tsp _lemon juice_, freshly squeezed
- 1 tbsp _extra virgin olive oil_
- 1/2 tsp _za'atar_ seasoning
- pinch of dried _oregano_

## Directions

1. Smash garlic into a smooth paste with a mortar and pestle.
2. Combine garlic paste, mayonnaise, yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, za'atar, and oregano in a small bowl. Mix until smooth.

## Notes

- **Storage:** Keep sealed in the fridge for up to 5-7 days.
