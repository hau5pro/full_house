## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup plain _whole milk yogurt_
- 1/4 cup _mayonnaise_
- 1 tbsp fresh _lemon juice_
- 1 _garlic_ clove, grated
- 1/2 tsp _onion powder_
- 1 tbsp chopped fresh _dill_

## Directions

1. Stir together yogurt, mayonnaise, lemon juice, garlic, onion powder, and dill in a small bowl.
2. Chill until ready to serve.
