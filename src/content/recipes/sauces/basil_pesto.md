## Ingredients

- 2 cups _fresh basil leaves_
- 3 cloves _garlic_, peeled
- 1/4 cup _pine nuts_ (or cashews)
- 1/2 cup _parmesan_, freshly grated
- 1/4 tsp _salt_
- 1/4 tsp _black pepper_
- 1 tsp _lemon juice_, fresh
- 1/2 cup _extra virgin olive oil_

## Directions

1. Pulse basil, garlic, pine nuts, and parmesan in a food processor until combined, about 1 min.
2. Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Pulse to incorporate.
3. Drizzle in olive oil on low speed until smooth, scraping down sides as needed.
4. Transfer to a bowl and serve.

## Notes

- **Storage:** Refrigerate in an airtight container up to 1 week.
- **Freezer:** Freeze in ice cube trays, then transfer to a freezer bag. Keeps up to 6 months.
