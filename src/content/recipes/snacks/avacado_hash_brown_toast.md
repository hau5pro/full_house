## Ingredients

- 2 ripe _avocados_
- 1/4 _red onion_, diced
- 1 _lime_, juiced
- 1 tsp _sriracha_
- 1/4 _jalapeño_, sliced into 4 discs
- 4 frozen _hash browns_
- _sea salt_ and _pepper_ to taste

## Directions

1. Cook hash browns according to package instructions until crispy.

2. Mash avocados, red onion, lime juice, sriracha, salt, and pepper with a fork until smooth.

3. Top each hash brown with guacamole and a jalapeño slice. Serve immediately.
