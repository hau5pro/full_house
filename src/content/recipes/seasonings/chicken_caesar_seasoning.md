## Ingredients

For 2 breasts:

- 1/2 tsp _garlic powder_
- 1/4 tsp _basil_
- 1/4 tsp _oregano_
- 1/4 tsp _black pepper_
- 1/4 tsp _paprika_
- 1/4 tsp _kosher salt_
- dash of _hickory liquid smoke_ (optional)

## Directions

1. Combine all spices in a small bowl and mix. Rub evenly over chicken breasts.
