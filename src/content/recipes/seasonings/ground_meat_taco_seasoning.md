## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp _chili powder_
- 1 tbsp _ground cumin_
- 2 tsp _cornstarch_
- 2 tsp _kosher salt_
- 2 tsp _mushroom powder_ (optional)
- 1 1/2 tsp _smoked paprika_
- 1 1/2 tsp _ground coriander_
- 1/2 tsp _cayenne pepper_ (to taste)

## Directions

1. Combine all spices in a bowl and mix well.
