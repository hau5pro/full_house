## Ingredients

- 4 medium _russet potatoes_
- _extra-virgin olive oil_
- _sea salt_

### Optional Toppings

- _butter_
- _sour cream_
- _shredded cheddar cheese_
- _bacon bits_
- _chives_, chopped

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Poke each potato several times with a fork.
3. Rub potatoes with olive oil and season generously with sea salt. Place on the baking sheet.
4. Bake 45–60 min, until fork-tender and skin is crisp.
5. Slice open, fluff the insides, and serve with desired toppings.
