## Ingredients

- 2 lb _baby bok choy_, halved or quartered
- 1 tbsp _vegetable oil_
- 5 cloves _garlic_, minced
- 2 large _shallots_, minced
- 2 tbsp _soy sauce_
- 1 tsp _sesame oil_
- 1 tsp _crushed red pepper_ (optional)

## Directions

1. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat, swirling to coat.
2. Add garlic and shallots; sauté, stirring constantly, 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
3. Add bok choy, soy sauce, and sesame oil; toss to coat, then cover.
4. Steam 1–2 minutes, uncover and toss, then re-cover and cook 3–5 more minutes until tender.
5. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes if using and serve immediately.

## Notes

- **Storage:** Refrigerate leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days; reheat in a hot skillet.
- **Substitution:** Regular bok choy works — quarter the heads and add 1–2 minutes to cook time.
