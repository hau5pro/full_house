## Ingredients

#### Original

- 1/4 cup _butter_
- 5 cups miniature _marshmallows_
- 6 cups _rice krispies cereal_
- 1/2 tsp _vanilla extract_ (optional)

#### Cinnamon Toast Crunch Variant

- 1/4 cup _butter_
- 5 cups miniature _marshmallows_
- 4 cups _cinnamon toast crunch_
- 2 cups _rice krispies cereal_
- 1 tsp _ground cinnamon_
- 1 tbsp _brown sugar_
- 1/2 tsp _vanilla extract_ (optional)

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat.
2. Add marshmallows and stir until fully melted. Remove from heat.
3. Stir in vanilla, then fold in cereal until well coated.
4. Press into a greased 13x9" pan with a buttered spatula.
5. Cool and cut into bars.
