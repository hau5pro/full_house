## Ingredients

- juice of 1 _lime_
- 1 packet _sweetener_
- 1 tsp _sugar_
- 1 cup _water_ (or _sparkling water_)

## Directions

1. Squeeze lime juice into a glass.
2. Add sweetener and sugar; stir until dissolved.
3. Add ice and water; stir to combine.

## Notes

- **Tartness:** Add lime juice by the tbsp until desired tartness is reached.
- **Sweetness:** If too sour, stir in more sweetener or a small drizzle of honey until dissolved.
- **Serving:** Best served super cold over plenty of ice.
- **Mixins:** Customize with a few fresh mint leaves or a drop of flavor enhancer or fruit concentrate.
