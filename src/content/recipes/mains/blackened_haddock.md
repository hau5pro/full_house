## Ingredients

- 12 oz _haddock fillets_, skinless, cut into halves or thirds
- 2 tbsp _olive oil_
- 4 _lemon_ slices or wedges

**Blackened Seasoning:**

- 1 tbsp _paprika_
- 1 tsp _cayenne pepper_
- 1 tsp _garlic powder_
- 1 tsp _black pepper_
- 1 tsp _salt_
- 1/2 tsp _cumin_
- 1/2 tsp _onion powder_
- 1/2 tsp _chili powder_
- 1/4 tsp _dried thyme_
- 1/4 tsp _dried oregano_
- 1/2 tsp _brown sugar_

## Directions

1. Mix all seasoning ingredients in a bowl, breaking up any lumps.
2. Press fillets firmly into the seasoning on both sides to coat.
3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high until shimmering. Add fillets and cook undisturbed for 2–3 minutes per side until blackened and internal temperature reaches 145°F.
4. Serve with lemon wedges.

## Notes

- **Chili powder:** Use a chili powder blend, not pure ground dried chilies.
- **Fish:** Fresh or fully thawed frozen haddock both work.
- **Serving:** Pairs well with buttered basmati rice.
