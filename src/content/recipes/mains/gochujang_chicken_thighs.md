## Ingredients

- 8–10 _boneless, skinless chicken thighs_
- 3–4 tbsp _gochujang_
- 1 tsp _gochugaru_ (Korean hot pepper powder)
- 2 1/2 tbsp _brown sugar_
- 2 tbsp _low-sodium soy sauce_
- 1 tbsp _sesame oil_
- 1 tsp _garlic powder_
- 1 tsp _onion powder_
- 1 tsp _paprika_
- 1/4 tsp _salt_
- 1/4 tsp _black pepper_

## Directions

1. Whisk together gochujang, gochugaru, brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
2. Coat chicken thighs in the marinade and marinate for 30 minutes.
3. Heat grill or grill pan to medium-high (about 400°F).
4. Cook smooth-side down for 4–5 minutes.
5. Flip and cook another 4–5 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
6. Rest under foil for 5–10 minutes before serving.

## Notes

- **Cast iron:** A cast iron skillet works as an alternative to a grill pan.
