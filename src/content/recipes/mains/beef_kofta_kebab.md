## Ingredients

- 4 _wooden or metal skewers_
- 1 lb _ground beef_ (85/15 or 80/20)
- 1 cup _onion_, finely minced
- 1 tsp _garlic_, finely minced
- 2 tsp _sweet paprika_
- 1 tsp _cumin_
- 1 tsp _turmeric_
- 1 tsp _sea salt_
- 1/4 tsp _black pepper_
- 1/4 tsp _red pepper flakes_
- 1/4 cup _fresh parsley_, chopped
- 1/4 cup _fresh cilantro_, chopped
- 1 tbsp _fresh mint_, chopped

## Directions

1. Whisk spices together in a small bowl.
2. Combine beef, spice blend, garlic, and onion in a large bowl.
3. Mix until combined. Cover and refrigerate 30 min.
4. Form beef into log shapes around skewers, pressing to adhere.
5. Grill over medium-high heat, turning once, 10-12 min total (165°F internal).

## Notes

- **Oven:** Bake at 350°F for 15-18 min.
- **Wooden skewers:** Soak in water 30 min before grilling over open flame.
