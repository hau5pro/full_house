## Ingredients

- 1 large _apple_
- 1 tbsp _unsalted butter_ (see note)
- 1 1/2 tbsp _honey_
- 1/4 tsp ground _cinnamon_
- 1 pinch _salt_
- _vanilla ice cream_

## Directions

1. Cut apple into small cubes, discarding core and seeds.
2. Melt butter in a medium pan over medium-high heat.
3. Add apple, honey, cinnamon, and salt. Stir to combine.
4. Cook 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until apples soften slightly.
5. Cool a couple minutes, then serve over vanilla ice cream.

## Notes

- **Tip:** If using salted butter, omit the pinch of salt.
